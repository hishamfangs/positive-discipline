Meet Nagwa

About Nagwa El Saadani

Nagwa is a certified Positive Discipline educator committed to helping parents nurture confident, cooperative children through playful learning and compassionate leadership.

  • Certified Positive Discipline Parent Educator
  • Serving families across Dubai & Cairo
  • Workshops tailored for toddlers to teens
  • Sessions in English & Arabic

Warm, respectful guidance for the whole family

With more than a decade of experience, Nagwa blends evidence-based tools with heartfelt facilitation. Her sessions invite laughter, reflection, and practical strategies so every parent leaves feeling supported and confident.

“When parents feel empowered, children flourish in connection and respect.” — Nagwa El Saadani
