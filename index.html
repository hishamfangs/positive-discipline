Meet Nagwa El Saadani

Positive Discipline Educator & Workshop Facilitator

Nagwa El Saadani is a certified Positive Discipline educator dedicated to supporting caregivers and teachers in cultivating resilient, emotionally intelligent communities. With over a decade of experience guiding families across the Middle East, she designs immersive learning spaces that celebrate kindness, clarity, and connection.

  • 12+ years guiding family-centric workshops
  • Bilingual delivery tailored to Dubai & Cairo audiences
  • Certified trainer with the Positive Discipline Association
“When adults feel supported, children feel seen. Positive Discipline is a journey of mutual respect, joy, and growth.”
